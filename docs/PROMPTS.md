# AI Prompt Templates

## Email Summarization
- Condense emails to 2-3 sentences
- Highlight deadlines, action items, important dates
- Flag priority based on content urgency

## Task Extraction
- Identify actionable items from emails
- Extract deadlines (explicit or inferred)
- Assign priority level

## Assistant Chat
- Context-aware: knows schedule, tasks, recent emails
- Proactive suggestions (reminders, scheduling)
- Natural language Q&A about campus life

## Proactive Alerts
- Deadline reminders (24h, 1h before)
- Schedule conflicts
- Unread important emails
- Suggested study blocks
